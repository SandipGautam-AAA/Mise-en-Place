# Mise en Place

> **"Everything in its place."**

## The Inital Concept
**Mise en Place** is a game centered on the art of preparation. It explores the meditative yet disciplined workflow of a professional kitchen, where the challenge lies not just in the cooking, but in the organization, timing, and precision of the workspace.

In a high-pressure environment, your success is determined by how well you prepare your station before the first order ever hits the rail.
